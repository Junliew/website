Favourite munchies!

No.6 Nutella Crepe

My favourite types of crepe tho will be a Nutella crepe! Nutella crepes are a delectable combination of French crepes and the irresistible chocolate hazelnut spread known as Nutella. These thin pancakes are filled with a generous amount of Nutella, creating a heavenly treat that will satisfy any sweet tooth. I like it when it is paired with banana, strawberry and ice-cream. I love the opposing delights of cold and warm when chewing through the soft and fluffy crepe with ice-cream with a hint of nutella. Mmhm--isn't that heavenly! My favourite Nutella crepe is the one from Harajuku, it will never go wrong for first tryers!

No.5 Banana crepe

If you're a banana lover you certainly should not ever miss out the banana crepe, usually sprinklled with almond crips and caramel, then often topped with luxurious cream. The natural sweetness of ripe bananas pairs perfectly with the delicate and slightly sweet crepe batter. The flavor profile is both comforting and indulgent! I especially loved the banana crepe from this crepe shop in Flinder Ln. named Roulgalette, they sell authentic French crepes, and they reaaly are heart warming on a cold day.

No.4 Choc-Lava cookie

What can be better than a chocolate chip cookie though, is a choclate lava cookie! These delicious chocolate lava cookies are chewy chocolate cookies, stuffed with molten chocolate ganache filling and sometimes sprinkled with powdered sugar. They're the perfect treat to make for the chocolate lover in your life!Chocolate lava cookies are often served warm, right out of the oven. The combination of warm cookie dough and the melted chocolate center gives a comforting feeling, making it a great treat for a cozy night in or during cold weather especially in Melbourne, its always my go-to comfort food!

No.3 Chocolate chip cookie

Now we talkin' about cookies. Cookies are my favourite desserts after I have super savoury lunch. I just think they always compliment the tastebuds well after having a heavy crash of salltiness, they my tummy feel a lil' better! Anyway, I gotta say my favourite cookie is the well-known classic--choclate chip cookie! Well, not just any chocolate chip cookie though! It has to be the choclate chip cookie from Subway! I prefer soft cookies with a warm,melt-in-your-mouth texture. It's a little piece of heaven that brings instant joy.The generous chunks of rich, velvety chocolate create bursts of indulgence with every bite. I feel like you can focus way more on the taste better than a crunchy cookie! Soft-cookies for the way!

No.2 Matcha cheesecake

Next off comes the matcha cheesecake from The Tokyo Restaurant in Kuala Lumpur,Malaysia. I have never thought of cheesecakes as a delight before I tried this one. The taste was seriously heavenly!!! It melted into my mouth and the whole thing is just.. soo delicious! Perfect for dessert! I did not know a cheese cake can be so fluffy. smooth and melty!! The sorbet beisde it is just ...chef's-kiss!! Honestly, I don't know how else to describe this other than 'heaven's cheesecake'! I would go back again and again just for the cheesecake, it isn't very cost effective but it really is worth the coin!

No.1 Watermelon cake

Watermelon cake is definitely one of the most memorable desserts I have tasted in my life! Ever since i came to Australia I was a little lost with where to search for amazing cakes! I havent't got to taste something that is significantly so tasteful that left its scent surrounding my mind for days! It was not until I finally visited Sydney! It was my first time meeting this precious watermelon cake combo! I have to say the combination of watermelon and cakes did seem intimidating to the mind, but I joke you not it taste so much better than how it looks! The crunch, the juiciness and then the smooth creamy texture altogether is a pleasant experience! The sweetness was naturally brought from the watermelon, all just right! Im glad the pastry shop is now at melbourne! Ya'll should go for a try!
